Introduction

I am a postdoctoral research scientist in the Tatonetti lab in the Department of Biomedical Informatics at Columbia University.

My research aims to discover biological phenomena by analyzing large-scale observational data sources. Prior to joining Biomedical Informatics, my research was in experimental particle physics. I was involved with the CDF collaboration at Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory and later the ATLAS collaboration at CERN.
